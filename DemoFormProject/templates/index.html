Contaminated land restoration issue

Yali's Flask Project

What is it?

Soil contamination is caused by the presence of xenobiotic chemicals (man-made) or some other change in the natural soil environment]. The main sources of land pollution are gas stations, industrial, transport, garages, building waste and pollution from defense industries.

Why I chose this topic?

I chose this topic because I was interested to see how the pollution in the land happens and how people treat it and restore the land.

How are the polluted soils restored?

Western soil remediation involves many professionals: hydrologists, geologists, soil engineers , building engineers, mechanical engineers, environmental engineers, architects , civil engineers and more. The soil treatments are divided into different types: biological treatment, thermal treatment, chemical treatment and combinations between the different treatments. There are many and varied technologies available to treat contaminated soils.
